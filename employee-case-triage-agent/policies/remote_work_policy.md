# Remote & Hybrid Work Policy

## Hybrid Baseline

Employees in hybrid roles work from the office at least 2 days per week, anchored on team days set by each department. Fully remote arrangements exist only where stated in the employment contract.

## Working From Abroad

Employees may work from another country for up to 20 working days per calendar year, subject to advance approval from People Operations for tax and right-to-work checks. Requests must be submitted at least 4 weeks before travel.

## Home Office Equipment

The company provides a laptop, monitor, keyboard and mouse for home use. A one-time home office stipend of 300 USD is available in an employee's first year, claimed through the expenses portal with receipts.

## Core Hours

Regardless of location, employees are expected to be reachable during core hours of 10:00–16:00 in their team's primary time zone.
