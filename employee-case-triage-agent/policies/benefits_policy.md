# Benefits Policy

## Health Insurance

All employees are enrolled in the company health plan from their first day. Dependants can be added during onboarding or at the annual enrolment window each November. Mid-year additions are only possible after a qualifying life event (marriage, birth, loss of other coverage) and must be requested within 30 days of the event.

## Retirement Plan

The company matches 401(k) contributions up to 4% of base salary. Matching vests immediately. Enrolment and contribution changes are made through the benefits portal at any time.

## Wellness Stipend

Employees receive a 50 USD monthly wellness stipend usable for gym memberships, fitness classes, or wellbeing apps, claimed through the expenses portal with receipts. Unused stipend does not roll over.

## Learning Budget

Each employee has an annual learning budget of 1,000 USD for courses, books and conferences, subject to manager approval.
