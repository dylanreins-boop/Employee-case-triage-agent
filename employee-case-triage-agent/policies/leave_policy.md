# Leave & Time Off Policy

## Annual Leave Entitlement

Full-time employees accrue 25 days of paid time off (PTO) per calendar year, accrued monthly at 2.08 days per month. Part-time employees accrue PTO pro-rata based on contracted hours.

## Carrying Over Unused PTO

Up to 5 unused PTO days may be carried over into the next calendar year. Carried-over days must be used by 31 March or they are forfeited. Carry-over beyond 5 days requires written approval from a department head and People Operations.

## Requesting Time Off

PTO requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 10 working days in advance for requests of 3 or more consecutive days. Shorter requests need 2 working days' notice. Approval is at the manager's discretion based on team coverage.

## Parental Leave

Primary caregivers are entitled to 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave; secondary caregivers to 4 weeks. Leave may be taken any time within 12 months of the birth or adoption. Employees must notify People Operations at least 8 weeks before the intended start date.

## Public Holidays

The company observes 9 public holidays per year, published each January. Employees required to work a public holiday receive a day in lieu.
