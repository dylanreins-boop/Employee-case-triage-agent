# Workplace Conduct Policy

## Respectful Workplace

The company is committed to a workplace free of harassment, bullying and discrimination. Concerns can be raised with a manager, any member of People Operations, or through the confidential ethics line. All reports are taken seriously and handled by trained HR staff — never by automated systems.

## Reporting Concerns

Reports of harassment, discrimination or retaliation are always reviewed by a human case manager within one business day. Reporters are protected from retaliation. Anonymous reports are accepted through the ethics line.

## Disciplinary Process

Disciplinary matters follow a documented process: informal conversation, written warning, final warning, termination. Each step is handled by the employee's manager together with a People Operations partner. Questions about an ongoing disciplinary matter should be directed to the assigned HR partner.
