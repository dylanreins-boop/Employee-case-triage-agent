# Expenses & Reimbursement Policy

## What Can Be Claimed

Reasonable business expenses are reimbursable: travel to client sites, accommodation for business trips, meals while travelling (up to the per diem), software or equipment pre-approved by a manager, and professional memberships relevant to the role.

## Per Diem Rates

Domestic travel: 60 USD per day for meals and incidentals. International travel: 85 USD per day. Per diems do not require receipts; all other claims do.

## Submitting a Claim

Expense claims are submitted through the finance portal within 60 days of the expense being incurred. Claims must include an itemised receipt (except per diems) and a business justification. Claims older than 60 days are rejected by default and require CFO approval.

## Reimbursement Timing

Approved claims are paid with the next payroll run. Claims approved after the 15th of the month are paid the following month.

## Non-Reimbursable Items

Alcohol (outside client entertainment approved in advance), commuting between home and the regular office, parking fines and traffic penalties, and personal entertainment are not reimbursable.
